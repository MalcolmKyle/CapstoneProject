About Us

Welcome to The Trading Post!
We are a small group of people that like to share and trade belongings, and now you can too!
The main premise is that you can look around trade items back and forth with anyone around the world.

A few key terms:

  • Item - can be an item you have or an item you need. Feel free to create a list of anything you have or need/want from someone.
    • Have - an item you have and wish to trade with someone.
    • Haves List - a list of your personal items you have and wish to trade
    • Need - an item you want to receive from someone
    • Needs List - a list of items you wish to receive.
  • Offer - specifics about terms of your personal offer or someone else's offer.
    • Accept - can initally accept an offer if you agree with the terms.
    • Counter - you can counter a user's offer with a counter offer.
    • Decline - if an offer is made on item you have, then you are free to decline the offer.
